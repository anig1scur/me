Yubeng, China ⛰️
altitude
3700 avg, 4500 max
period
2022.12.31 - 2023.01.06
Although I hadn't fully recovered from Covid that I caught two weeks prior, I enthusiastically accepted my colleagues' invitation to hike in Yubeng.
Thanks to my colleagues' planning, I didn't need to do any preparation or research about this place. However, it turned out to be an incredible first multi-day trekking experience for me.
My favorite part of the trip was hiking the Chongcao route. Under clear skies, I was deeply moved by the surrounding snow-capped mountains. Even now, I can still recall the crisp, cold air at the mountain peak.
